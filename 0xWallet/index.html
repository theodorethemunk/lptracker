About us

LpTracker is a unique bot tracker that gives users the ability to track liquidity pool burning, locked liquidity pools, percentages of burned liquidity, and whether or not a pool of liquidity has been burned.

LpTracker is a special bot that reveals Scam developers by disclosing the overall percentage of burned liquidity that has been carried out by the developers as well as how the developers have been deceiving investors about burned liquidity. It guarantees the security of investment money.

In order to fully utilize LpTracker, you must join the official LpTracker channel. LpTracker may be added to groups for simple usage to validate LP locked, LP burned, and % of LP burned.

Important Features

- Confirmation of lp burn: When a project's contract interacts with LpTracker, it verifies if a project has completed the liquidity burn transactions in the affirmative or negative.


- Burn % for LP : When LpTracker interacts with a project's contract address, it displays the overall percentage that has been burned.

- Lp lock duration: When a contract address interacts with LpTracker, it displays how long a project's locking period is.

- Disclosed lp locker: When LpTracker interacts with a project's contract address, it displays the lock where the liquidity is being locked.
